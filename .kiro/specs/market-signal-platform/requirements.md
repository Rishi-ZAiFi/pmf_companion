# Requirements Document

## Introduction

The Market Signal Platform is a product-market fit (PMF) intelligence service for startup founders. It collects and analyzes market signals through two layers: **active signals** (AI-powered conversations via voice, email, SMS, and chat) and **passive signals** (automated scraping of Reddit, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Hacker News, Product Hunt, and review sites). Founders receive a unified signal feed, a live PMF score, persona maps, and competitor gap analysis — enabling continuous, data-driven product validation without manual research effort.

---

## Glossary

- **Platform**: The Market Signal Platform web application and its backend services
- **Founder**: A startup founder or product manager who has registered an account on the Platform
- **Project**: A workspace scoped to a single product, containing its own signal feed, PMF score, ICP definition, and campaign history
- **ICP**: Ideal Customer Profile — the target user persona defined by the Founder for a given Project
- **Signal**: A discrete unit of market intelligence, sourced from either active conversations or passive internet scraping
- **Active Signal**: A Signal collected through a Platform-initiated AI conversation with a contact
- **Passive Signal**: A Signal collected through automated scraping of public internet sources
- **Campaign**: A targeted outreach effort that sends AI-driven conversations to a set of contacts via one or more channels
- **Contact**: A person (name, email, and/or phone number) uploaded or imported by the Founder as a potential interview subject
- **PMF Score**: A numeric score from 0 to 100 representing the degree of product-market fit, derived from active survey responses using the Sean Ellis methodology
- **Theme Cluster**: A named group of semantically related Signals, generated by the Platform using embedding-based clustering and LLM summarization
- **Signal Feed**: The ranked, deduplicated, unified list of all Signals for a given Project
- **Voice Agent**: The AI-powered voice bot that conducts outbound and inbound phone interviews on behalf of the Founder
- **Scraper**: The Platform subsystem responsible for collecting Passive Signals from external internet sources
- **Embedding Engine**: The Platform subsystem that generates vector embeddings for Signals and performs semantic clustering
- **Transcript**: The text record of a voice or chat conversation, produced by the Platform after a call or session ends
- **Relevance Score**: A numeric value from 0 to 100 assigned to each Signal indicating how closely it matches the Project's ICP and problem space

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Founder Onboarding and Project Setup

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want to create an account and define my product context, so that the Platform can immediately begin collecting relevant market signals.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL allow a Founder to register using an email address and password or via Google OAuth.
2. WHEN a Founder completes registration, THE Platform SHALL prompt the Founder to create a first Project before displaying any other screen.
3. THE Platform SHALL require each Project to capture the following fields before activation: product name, one-line product description, target ICP description, primary problem being solved, and up to five competitor names.
4. WHEN a Founder saves a Project, THE Platform SHALL use the Project's ICP description and problem statement to generate an initial set of scraping keywords and subreddit candidates within 60 seconds.
5. THE Platform SHALL allow a Founder to create, archive, and delete Projects at any time.
6. WHILE a Project is archived, THE Platform SHALL suspend all scraping and campaign activity for that Project and retain its historical Signal data.
7. THE Platform SHALL allow a Founder to manage multiple Projects from a single account, up to the limit defined by the Founder's billing plan.

---

### Requirement 2: Passive Signal Collection — Reddit

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want the Platform to monitor Reddit for relevant discussions, so that I can discover pain points and competitor mentions without manually browsing subreddits.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Project is activated, THE Scraper SHALL identify and begin monitoring at least five relevant subreddits based on the Project's ICP and problem statement.
2. THE Scraper SHALL ingest new Reddit posts and comments matching the Project's keyword set at intervals no greater than 6 hours.
3. THE Platform SHALL allow a Founder to manually add or remove subreddits from a Project's monitoring list at any time.
4. WHEN a Reddit post or comment is ingested, THE Platform SHALL classify it as one of the following signal types: pain point, feature request, competitor mention, market trend, positive sentiment, or negative sentiment.
5. WHEN a Reddit post or comment is ingested, THE Platform SHALL assign it a Relevance Score between 0 and 100 based on semantic similarity to the Project's ICP and problem statement.
6. IF a Reddit post or comment has already been ingested for the same Project, THEN THE Scraper SHALL discard the duplicate without creating a new Signal record.

---

### Requirement 3: Passive Signal Collection — Twitter/X

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want the Platform to monitor Twitter/X for relevant conversations, so that I can track real-time sentiment and competitor activity.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Project is activated, THE Scraper SHALL begin monitoring Twitter/X for tweets and threads matching the Project's keywords, hashtags, and competitor handles.
2. THE Scraper SHALL ingest new Twitter/X signals at intervals no greater than 30 minutes.
3. THE Platform SHALL track sentiment trend per keyword cluster over time, storing at minimum a daily sentiment aggregate per cluster.
4. WHEN a tweet or thread is ingested, THE Platform SHALL classify it using the same signal type taxonomy defined in Requirement 2, Criterion 4.
5. IF a tweet or thread has already been ingested for the same Project, THEN THE Scraper SHALL discard the duplicate without creating a new Signal record.

---

### Requirement 4: Passive Signal Collection — Hacker News

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want the Platform to monitor Hacker News for relevant posts and comments, so that I can capture technical and founder community perspectives on my problem space.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Project is activated, THE Scraper SHALL begin monitoring Hacker News "Ask HN" and "Show HN" posts relevant to the Project's problem space.
2. THE Scraper SHALL ingest new Hacker News signals at intervals no greater than 2 hours.
3. WHEN a Hacker News post or comment is ingested, THE Platform SHALL analyze it for pain points, opinions, and competitor mentions and classify it using the signal type taxonomy defined in Requirement 2, Criterion 4.
4. IF a Hacker News post or comment has already been ingested for the same Project, THEN THE Scraper SHALL discard the duplicate without creating a new Signal record.

---

### Requirement 5: Passive Signal Collection — LinkedIn

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want the Platform to monitor LinkedIn for relevant posts and job listings, so that I can identify growth signals and market trends among target companies.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Project is activated, THE Scraper SHALL begin monitoring public LinkedIn posts and articles matching the Project's ICP and problem space.
2. THE Scraper SHALL monitor job postings from companies matching the Project's ICP as hiring and growth signals.
3. THE Scraper SHALL refresh LinkedIn signals at intervals no greater than 24 hours.
4. WHEN a LinkedIn post, article, or job posting is ingested, THE Platform SHALL classify it using the signal type taxonomy defined in Requirement 2, Criterion 4.
5. IF a LinkedIn item has already been ingested for the same Project, THEN THE Scraper SHALL discard the duplicate without creating a new Signal record.

---

### Requirement 6: Passive Signal Collection — Review Sites

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want the Platform to scrape competitor reviews from G2, Trustpilot, Product Hunt, and app stores, so that I can identify unmet needs and competitor weaknesses.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Project is activated and competitor names are defined, THE Scraper SHALL begin collecting reviews for those competitors from G2, Trustpilot, Product Hunt, and the Apple App Store and Google Play Store where listings exist.
2. THE Scraper SHALL refresh review site signals at intervals no greater than 7 days.
3. WHEN a review is ingested, THE Platform SHALL classify it using the signal type taxonomy defined in Requirement 2, Criterion 4.
4. WHEN a review is classified as negative sentiment or competitor weakness, THE Platform SHALL flag it as an opportunity signal in the Signal Feed.
5. IF a review has already been ingested for the same Project, THEN THE Scraper SHALL discard the duplicate without creating a new Signal record.

---

### Requirement 7: Signal Processing and Semantic Clustering

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want all signals to be automatically deduplicated, classified, and grouped into themes, so that I can quickly understand patterns without reading every individual signal.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Signal is ingested from any source, THE Embedding Engine SHALL generate a vector embedding for the Signal's text content within 5 minutes of ingestion.
2. THE Embedding Engine SHALL cluster Signals into Theme Clusters using semantic similarity, grouping Signals whose embeddings are within a cosine similarity threshold of 0.80.
3. WHEN a Theme Cluster reaches 5 or more Signals, THE Platform SHALL generate a human-readable cluster name and summary using an LLM.
4. THE Platform SHALL update Theme Cluster membership and summaries at intervals no greater than 1 hour as new Signals are ingested.
5. WHEN a Signal is assigned a Relevance Score below 20, THE Platform SHALL exclude it from the Signal Feed but retain it in storage for audit purposes.
6. FOR ALL Signals ingested from the same source URL within the same Project, THE Platform SHALL store exactly one Signal record (round-trip deduplication property).

### Requirement 8: Contact Management

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want to import and manage a list of contacts, so that I can target specific users for AI-driven interview campaigns.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL allow a Founder to upload a CSV file containing contacts with at minimum the following fields: first name, last name, email address, and phone number.
2. WHEN a CSV is uploaded, THE Platform SHALL validate each row and report the count of valid and invalid rows to the Founder before importing.
3. IF a CSV row is missing both an email address and a phone number, THEN THE Platform SHALL mark that row as invalid and exclude it from the import.
4. THE Platform SHALL allow a Founder to connect a HubSpot, Intercom, or Mailchimp account to import contacts from those systems.
5. THE Platform SHALL allow a Founder to assign one or more segment tags to each Contact (for example: power user, churned, trial, prospect).
6. THE Platform SHALL allow a Founder to add, edit, and remove Contacts and segment tags at any time.

---

### Requirement 9: Campaign Creation and Management

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want to create outreach campaigns that send AI-driven conversations to my contacts, so that I can collect structured feedback at scale.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL allow a Founder to create a Campaign by specifying: a name, a target contact segment, one or more delivery channels, and a campaign goal.
2. THE Platform SHALL support the following campaign goals: PMF survey, pain point discovery, feature validation, and churn reason investigation.
3. THE Platform SHALL support the following delivery channels: email, SMS, voice call, and in-app chat widget.
4. WHEN a Campaign is created, THE Platform SHALL auto-generate a conversation script and AI bot persona based on the campaign goal and the Project's product description and ICP.
5. THE Platform SHALL allow a Founder to review and edit the auto-generated conversation script before the Campaign is launched.
6. WHEN a Founder launches a Campaign, THE Platform SHALL begin delivering conversations to the target contacts within 15 minutes.
7. THE Platform SHALL allow a Founder to pause, resume, or cancel a Campaign at any time.
8. WHILE a Campaign is paused, THE Platform SHALL not initiate new conversations but SHALL allow in-progress conversations to complete.

---

### Requirement 10: Email Channel

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want the Platform to send personalized emails and handle replies with AI, so that I can conduct asynchronous interviews via email without manual effort.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL allow a Founder to configure a sender email address and display name for outbound campaign emails.
2. WHEN a Campaign email is sent, THE Platform SHALL personalize the email body with the Contact's first name and any available segment context.
3. WHEN a Contact replies to a campaign email, THE Platform SHALL route the reply to the AI conversation engine and send a follow-up response within 5 minutes.
4. THE Platform SHALL allow the AI conversation engine to send up to 5 follow-up messages per Contact per Campaign before closing the thread.
5. WHEN an email thread is closed, THE Platform SHALL generate a Transcript and extract key insights, sentiment, and pain intensity score (1–10) from the thread.
6. IF a Contact replies with an opt-out phrase (for example: "unsubscribe", "stop", "remove me"), THEN THE Platform SHALL immediately cease all email outreach to that Contact and mark the Contact as opted out.

---

### Requirement 11: SMS and Chat Channel

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want the Platform to conduct AI conversations via SMS and a web chat widget, so that I can reach users on the channels they prefer.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL send outbound SMS messages to Contacts using a Twilio-provisioned phone number.
2. WHEN a Contact replies to an SMS, THE Platform SHALL route the reply to the AI conversation engine and send a follow-up response within 2 minutes.
3. THE Platform SHALL provide a JavaScript embed snippet that a Founder can install on any web page to activate the in-app chat widget.
4. WHEN a user interacts with the chat widget, THE Platform SHALL initiate an AI-driven conversation based on the active Campaign configuration for that Project.
5. THE Platform SHALL allow the AI conversation engine to send up to 8 follow-up messages per Contact per SMS or chat session before closing the session.
6. WHEN a session is closed, THE Platform SHALL generate a Transcript and extract key insights, sentiment, and pain intensity score (1–10) from the session.
7. IF a Contact replies to an SMS with an opt-out phrase (for example: "STOP", "UNSUBSCRIBE"), THEN THE Platform SHALL immediately cease all SMS outreach to that Contact and mark the Contact as opted out.

---

### Requirement 12: Voice Call Channel

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want the Platform to place AI-powered voice calls to my contacts, so that I can conduct natural-language interviews at scale without scheduling calls myself.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a voice Campaign is launched, THE Voice Agent SHALL place outbound calls to target Contacts using a Vapi or Bland AI integration.
2. THE Voice Agent SHALL conduct the call as a natural, open-ended conversation — asking follow-up questions, probing vague answers, and exploring pain points — rather than reading a rigid script.
3. THE Voice Agent SHALL ask "why" or an equivalent probing question at least once per conversation turn where the Contact's response is fewer than 15 words.
4. THE Platform SHALL record all voice calls with the Contact's consent, obtained via a verbal disclosure at the start of each call.
5. WHEN a call ends, THE Platform SHALL produce a Transcript of the call within 5 minutes.
6. WHEN a Transcript is produced, THE Platform SHALL extract: key quotes, sentiment, pain intensity score (1–10), willingness-to-pay signals, and competitor mentions.
7. THE Platform SHALL allow a Founder to listen to call recordings and read Transcripts from the Project dashboard.
8. IF a Contact declines to participate or requests to end the call, THEN THE Voice Agent SHALL end the call immediately and mark the Contact as opted out of voice outreach.

---

### Requirement 13: Conversation Intelligence

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want all AI conversations to probe deeply and extract structured insights, so that I get high-quality signal rather than surface-level responses.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL analyze every completed Transcript for the following attributes: overall sentiment (positive, neutral, negative), pain intensity score (1–10), willingness-to-pay signals (present or absent), and competitor mentions (list of names).
2. WHEN a Transcript is analyzed, THE Platform SHALL merge the extracted insights into the Project's Signal Feed as one or more Active Signals.
3. THE Embedding Engine SHALL generate embeddings for Active Signals derived from Transcripts and cluster them with Passive Signals into unified Theme Clusters.
4. THE Platform SHALL surface the top 3 verbatim quotes from each Transcript as representative quotes in the Signal Feed entry for that conversation.

---

### Requirement 14: Unified Signal Feed

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want a single ranked feed of all signals from every source, so that I can review the most important market intelligence in one place.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL display a Signal Feed for each Project that combines Active Signals and Passive Signals in a single ranked list.
2. THE Platform SHALL rank Signals in the Signal Feed by a composite score derived from Relevance Score, recency, and signal type weight.
3. THE Platform SHALL load the Signal Feed and display the first 50 results within 2 seconds of the Founder opening the dashboard.
4. THE Platform SHALL allow a Founder to filter the Signal Feed by: source (Reddit, Twitter/X, HN, LinkedIn, review sites, email, SMS, voice, widget), signal type, date range, sentiment, and Relevance Score threshold.
5. THE Platform SHALL allow a Founder to bookmark, tag with a custom label, or dismiss any Signal.
6. WHEN a Founder selects a Signal, THE Platform SHALL display a link to the original source (for example: the Reddit post URL, the call Transcript, or the review page).

---

### Requirement 15: PMF Score

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want a live PMF score derived from my active survey responses, so that I can track product-market fit over time with a single metric.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL calculate the PMF Score as the percentage of active survey respondents who answer "very disappointed" when asked how they would feel if they could no longer use the product, following the Sean Ellis methodology.
2. THE Platform SHALL display the PMF Score prominently on the Project dashboard, updated within 1 hour of each new survey response being processed.
3. THE Platform SHALL display a PMF Score trend chart showing score history at daily granularity for the past 90 days.
4. THE Platform SHALL break down the PMF Score by Contact segment, displaying a per-segment score alongside the overall score.
5. WHEN the PMF Score changes by 5 or more points within a 24-hour period, THE Platform SHALL send an alert to the Founder via email and in-app notification.
6. WHERE fewer than 40 survey responses have been collected, THE Platform SHALL display the PMF Score with a confidence warning indicating the sample size is insufficient for statistical reliability.

---

### Requirement 16: Theme Clusters Dashboard

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want to see signals grouped into named themes with trend indicators, so that I can quickly identify the most important patterns in my market data.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL display all Theme Clusters for a Project on a dedicated dashboard view, sorted by Signal count descending.
2. WHEN a Theme Cluster is displayed, THE Platform SHALL show: the cluster name, Signal count, trend direction (growing, stable, or declining) based on Signal ingestion rate over the past 7 days, and up to 3 representative quotes.
3. THE Platform SHALL allow a Founder to click into a Theme Cluster to view all Signals within that cluster.
4. THE Platform SHALL allow a Founder to rename, merge, or dismiss Theme Clusters.
5. WHEN a new Theme Cluster reaches 10 or more Signals, THE Platform SHALL send an in-app notification to the Founder.

---

### Requirement 17: Persona Map

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want the Platform to identify distinct user personas from my signal data, so that I can understand who my users are and what they care about.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL identify distinct user personas by clustering Contacts and their associated Signal patterns using the Embedding Engine.
2. WHEN a persona is identified, THE Platform SHALL display: a generated persona name, a description of who they are, their primary pain points, their average pain intensity score, and their PMF likelihood (high, medium, or low).
3. THE Platform SHALL derive personas from both Active Signal conversation data and Passive Signal language patterns where Contact attribution is available.
4. THE Platform SHALL refresh persona definitions at intervals no greater than 24 hours as new Signals are ingested.
5. WHERE fewer than 10 Contacts have contributed Signals, THE Platform SHALL display a notice that persona data is insufficient and suppress persona generation.

---

### Requirement 18: Competitor Gap Analysis

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want to see aggregated competitor weaknesses and unmet market needs, so that I can identify positioning opportunities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL aggregate competitor weakness Signals from review sites and social sources into a competitor gap map for each Project.
2. THE Platform SHALL display the gap map as a list of unmet needs, each showing: the need description, the number of supporting Signals, and the competitor(s) most frequently associated with that gap.
3. THE Platform SHALL allow a Founder to add or remove tracked competitors for a Project at any time.
4. WHEN a competitor is removed from a Project, THE Platform SHALL retain historical Signals attributed to that competitor but cease collecting new Signals for that competitor.

---

### Requirement 19: Alerts and Notifications

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want to receive timely alerts about significant changes in my signal data, so that I can respond quickly to important market developments.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the PMF Score changes by 5 or more points within a 24-hour period, THE Platform SHALL notify the Founder via email and in-app notification within 1 hour of the change being detected.
2. WHEN a new Theme Cluster reaches 10 or more Signals within a 48-hour window, THE Platform SHALL send an in-app notification to the Founder.
3. THE Platform SHALL send a weekly digest email to the Founder every Monday at 09:00 in the Founder's configured timezone, summarizing: top 5 Signals by Relevance Score, PMF Score movement, and new Theme Clusters from the past 7 days.
4. THE Platform SHALL allow a Founder to configure notification preferences, including disabling individual notification types.
5. IF a Founder has not configured a timezone, THEN THE Platform SHALL default to UTC for scheduled notifications.

---

### Requirement 20: Integrations

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want the Platform to connect with my existing tools, so that I can incorporate market signals into my existing workflows.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHERE a Founder has connected a Slack workspace, THE Platform SHALL post Signal summaries and PMF Score alerts to a Founder-configured Slack channel.
2. WHERE a Founder has connected a HubSpot or Intercom account, THE Platform SHALL sync Contact records and push Signal-derived tags back to the connected CRM.
3. WHERE a Founder has connected a Notion workspace, THE Platform SHALL allow the Founder to export Signal reports and Theme Cluster summaries as Notion pages.
4. THE Platform SHALL provide a webhook configuration interface allowing a Founder to register up to 10 webhook endpoints that receive a POST request when a new Signal is created or the PMF Score changes.
5. WHERE a Founder has connected a Segment source, THE Platform SHALL receive user behavior events from Segment and use them to trigger in-app widget Campaign conversations.

---

### Requirement 21: Billing and Plan Enforcement

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want clear plan tiers with enforced limits, so that I can choose the right plan for my usage and upgrade when needed.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL enforce the following plan limits:
   - Free tier: 1 Project, passive signals only, 50 active conversations per calendar month
   - Starter ($49/month): 3 Projects, all channels, 500 active conversations per calendar month
   - Growth ($149/month): 10 Projects, all channels, 2,000 active conversations per calendar month, Slack and CRM integrations enabled
   - Enterprise: unlimited Projects, all channels, unlimited conversations, all integrations, custom AI persona training
2. WHEN a Founder on the Free tier attempts to launch an active Campaign, THE Platform SHALL display an upgrade prompt and prevent the Campaign from launching.
3. WHEN a Founder reaches 90% of their monthly active conversation limit, THE Platform SHALL send an email and in-app notification warning the Founder of the approaching limit.
4. WHEN a Founder reaches 100% of their monthly active conversation limit, THE Platform SHALL pause all active Campaigns and notify the Founder.
5. THE Platform SHALL process all subscription payments and plan changes via Stripe.
6. WHEN a payment fails, THE Platform SHALL notify the Founder via email and downgrade the account to the Free tier after a 7-day grace period if payment is not resolved.

---

### Requirement 22: Security and Compliance

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want the Platform to handle user data securely and in compliance with privacy regulations, so that I can use it without legal risk.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL encrypt all stored user data, Contact records, Signal data, and call recordings using AES-256 encryption at rest.
2. THE Platform SHALL transmit all data between clients and the Platform using TLS 1.2 or higher.
3. WHEN a Founder launches a Campaign, THE Platform SHALL require the Founder to confirm that they have obtained consent from their Contacts to be contacted by the Platform on the Founder's behalf.
4. THE Platform SHALL provide a data deletion endpoint allowing a Founder to permanently delete all data associated with their account within 30 days of a deletion request.
5. THE Platform SHALL provide a Contact opt-out mechanism accessible via a link in every outbound email and SMS, allowing Contacts to opt out of all future outreach from a given Project.
6. THE Platform SHALL maintain an audit log of all data access and export events for a minimum of 90 days.

---

### Requirement 23: Performance and Availability

**User Story:** As a Founder, I want the Platform to be fast and reliable, so that I can depend on it for ongoing market research.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Platform SHALL maintain a monthly uptime of 99.5% or greater, excluding scheduled maintenance windows communicated at least 24 hours in advance.
2. THE Platform SHALL load the Signal Feed dashboard and display the first 50 results within 2 seconds under normal load conditions (defined as fewer than 1,000 concurrent users).
3. THE Platform SHALL complete voice call transcription and deliver the Transcript to the dashboard within 5 minutes of a call ending.
4. THE Scraper SHALL respect the robots.txt directives and published rate limits of all scraped platforms, using rotating proxies where necessary to avoid IP-based blocking.
5. WHEN a third-party scraping source returns an error or rate-limit response, THE Scraper SHALL implement exponential backoff with a minimum initial delay of 60 seconds before retrying.
